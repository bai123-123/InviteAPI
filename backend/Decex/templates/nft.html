NFT Artist

Zemm

Zemm has been making art about cryptocurrencies since 2017. Recently, he illustrated for Yearn Finance' yGift project and has sold out several series of NFTs on Rarible. Zemm is known for melding dexterous pen drawing with digital graphics and animations to articulate and explore the mythos of emergent technology and shifting cultures in the crypto sphere.

NFT Collections

 DeCEX Genesis

The imagery of DeCEX is paired with an abstracted icon of twin sea-nymphs, who hold a water flask. The ocean has always been considered a place of generative aspects, which is the metaphor of DeCEX's tenacious vitality at the time of project launch.

The Genesis NFT can be only obtained in the Public Sale Event.

This collection presents portraits of icons and influencers in the crypto industry. It combines the dynamic background and abstract elements in the NFT designs.
